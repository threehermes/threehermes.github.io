About Three Hermes

Three Hermes is a publishing house that aims to put back in print historic works that are, today, mostly only known to scholars, often forgotten and inaccessible by the wider public. We have a focus on philosophy, meta-physics, magic, and religion.

The Lapidary of Damigeron

Edited by Emilio Alexandrescu
Damigeron's Lapidary is a famous ancient lapidary, thought to be 2000 years old, which, in this edition, contains 50 stones, together with their descriptions, magical virtues, some talismans, and more. This work has been translated from the 1881 Latin edition of Eugenius Abel, who used multiple manuscripts to compile his version and aimed to publish the book without the omissions made by the people before him, and includes over 60 footnotes by him, together with his introduction.
